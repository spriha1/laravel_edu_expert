1.  Code uses 4 spaces for indenting, not tabs.
2.  Opening braces for classes goes on the next line, and closing braces goes on the next line after 
    the body.
3.  Opening braces for methods goes on the next line, and closing braces goes on the next line after 
	the body.
4.  Control structure keywords have one space after them, method and function calls do not have.
5.  Opening braces for control structures goes on the same line, and closing braces goes on the next line 
    after the body.
6.  Opening parentheses for control structures do not have a space after them, and closing parentheses 
	for control structures do not have a space before.
7.  Operators have a space before and after them.
